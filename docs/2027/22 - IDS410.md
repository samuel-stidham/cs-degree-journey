**Course Description:**
In this culminating experience, students will apply the knowledge, skills, and abilities developed throughout their various course experiences to address an opportunity for social change. Through the examination of a complex community- or workplace-based issue, students plan for and advocate a feasible intervention that aims to improve socially just and equitable outcomes across varying impacted contexts, environments, and populations.
